## Error Found and Corrected

### VMU Flash Memory (Footnote 31)
- **Claim**: "16 KB ROM, and 64 KB Flash memory"
- **Correct**: "16 KB ROM, and 128 KB Flash memory"
- **Source**: Wikipedia VMU article; VMU has 128 KB total flash (28 KB reserved for system, 100 KB user-accessible = 200 blocks × 512 bytes)
- **Action**: Corrected footnote 31 from "64 KB Flash memory" to "128 KB Flash memory"
